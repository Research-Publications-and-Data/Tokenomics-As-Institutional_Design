    Jul 2025 s2r_clean = 0.0737 (spot, not rolling)

      the spot value. As long as the paper is citing the rolling average,
      this is consistent.

  PAPER CLAIM: "Feb 2026 fiscal parity ~1.84" (§6.1, B3 cite)
    Feb 2026 s2r_clean = 2.0597
    Feb 2026 s2r_3m_rolling = 1.5025
    Feb 2026 s2r_median_5m = 1.5517
    Paper says: 1.84
    DISCREPANCY: Data shows 2.060, paper cites 1.84 (data is 11.9% higher)
    → No smoothing method in the file yields 1.84. The spot value (2.06),

      a different burns definition (e.g., excluding large-signer burns).
    Jan 2026 s2r_clean = 1.1027 (too low). Oct 2025 = 1.5696, Nov = 0.8773,
      Dec = 1.5517. None match 1.84 cleanly.
    STATUS: ⚠ DISCREPANCY — paper cites 1.84, data shows 2.060 for Feb 2026.
            Paper should be updated to 2.06 or the exact computation
            method used to derive 1.84 identified and documented.

CHECK 4: GEODNET Burns/Emissions
(data/raw/geodnet_monthly_burns.csv + geodnet_monthly_emissions.csv)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Burns file:     19 months (Sep 2024 – Mar 2026)
  Emissions file: 19 months (Sep 2024 – Mar 2026) ✓ aligned

  PAPER CLAIM: "GEODNET S2R = 0.379" (§3.4, H3.3)
    Overall S2R (total burns / total emissions, all 19 months): 0.0690
    → The aggregate is dominated by Sep–Oct 2025 emission spikes:
        Sep 2025: 34.9M emitted, only 10 burned (S2R ≈ 0)
        Oct 2025: 101.1M emitted, 176K burned (S2R = 0.002)
      These two months account for 62% of total emissions.

    Monthly S2R values for window closest to 0.379:
      Dec 2024–Jun 2025 average: 0.3718 ≈ 0.379 ✓ (7-month window)
      Oct 2024–Aug 2025 average: 0.2748 (11-month window, farther)
    → The 0.379 claim is approximately valid for the Dec 2024–Jun 2025
      window (mean 0.372, vs cited 0.379; delta = −0.007).
    → However: the paper does not specify this window. The post-Jun 2025
      collapse (Sep 2025 near-zero S2R) renders the 0.379 figure
      misleading if presented as current or representative.
    STATUS: ⚠ WINDOW-DEPENDENT — S2R = 0.379 is approximately supported
            for Dec 2024–Jun 2025 only. Paper must specify this window
            explicitly. The full-dataset S2R is 0.069.

  PAPER CLAIM: "burns absorbing 36% of monthly emissions" (§3.4)
    Feb 2026 burn/emission ratio: 1,336,932 / 3,695,433 = 36.2% ✓
    → This claim precisely matches the Feb 2026 data point.
    → Note: Mar 2026 drops to 0.5%, showing high volatility.
    STATUS: CONFIRMED for Feb 2026 specifically.

  REGRESSION DATA NOTE:
    regression_data_april2026.csv lists GEODNET subsidy_ratio_onchain = 2.64.
    This differs from both the paper's 0.379 and the computed 0.069.
    The 2.64 figure uses a different denominator (likely revenue_usd, not
    token-denominated emissions). The metrics are not directly comparable.
    Revenue_annual_usd for GEODNET in regression = $9,194,616 (defillama_fees_proxy).
